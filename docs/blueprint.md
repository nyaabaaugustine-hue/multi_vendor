# **App Name**: VaultCommerce

## Core Features:

- User Account & Profile Management: Users (e.g., Employers, Employees, Landlords, Tenants, Teachers, Students, Buyers, Sellers) can register, create profiles, and manage their personal and business information.
- Cross-Category Listing Management: Providers can list products or services across various categories, including detailed descriptions, pricing, and media.
- Browse and Search Functionality: Users can efficiently browse categories and search for specific products, services, or providers on the marketplace.
- Transaction & Escrow Initiation: Facilitate the initiation of a transaction where funds are captured and securely held in an escrow vault via Paystack upon agreement.
- Proof of Delivery/Service Confirmation: Buyers/recipients can confirm the satisfactory completion of delivery or service, triggering the next stage of fund release.
- Automated Fund Disbursement: After verification, the system automatically executes a split-payment logic, distributing net revenue to the provider and commission to the platform treasury via Paystack.
- AI-Powered Listing Assistant Tool: A generative AI tool that assists providers in creating compelling and optimized descriptions for their product or service listings.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, professional blue (#16599C) symbolizing trust, stability, and reliability. This will be used for main interactive elements, headers, and branding.
- Background color: A very light, cool-toned off-white (#F0F2F5) providing a clean, airy, and unobtrusive canvas that ensures readability and a sense of calm efficiency.
- Accent color: A vibrant aqua (#0DEDE6) that provides a dynamic and contemporary touch, drawing attention to key actions, statuses, and highlights, ensuring clear visual hierarchy.
- Headline and Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clear, and objective aesthetic, ensuring excellent readability for all transaction details and marketplace content.
- Use modern, clean, and line-based icons for clarity and intuitive navigation, reflecting concepts like security, transactions, listings, and user profiles.
- Emphasize a clean, organized, and responsive layout. Critical transaction steps and escrow statuses should be prominently displayed to reinforce transparency and trust.
- Implement subtle and purposeful animations, particularly to provide immediate feedback on user actions related to transactions and escrow fund movements, enhancing perceived reliability.
